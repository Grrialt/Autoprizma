About Autoprizma

UAB Autoprizma is young but qualified and dynamic company that provides transportation services.
We transport cargo throughout Europe, as well as from Europe / to Europe.
We aim for common goals, shared success and mutual benefits.

This is derived using our best qualities:

  • Professionalism.

    We guarantee high quality service and energetic, hardworking team.

  • Reliability.

    Our commitments are proven by our deeds.

  • Flexibility.

    We provide customized and versatile client-oriented solutions.

  • Constantly growing competence.

    We are maintaining permanent study, advanced training, innovation and practice.
