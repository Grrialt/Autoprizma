Our services

We transport goods of various sizes throughout Europe and transport import-export goods between Europe and Lithuania. For transportation planning and management, we always use the latest information technologies and technical means, covering route planning, management, control and accounting..

Timely and safe delivery of goods by our transport is the main condition for the provision of our services.

Services offered:

  • transportation of full and partial cargo in the Europe;

  • transportation of full, partial and small cargo to Russia and Belarus;

  • delivery in Lithuania and Baltic countries;

  • performance of customs formalities;

  • customs clearance of cargo in Russia;

  • cargo tracking using GPS devices;

  • the ability to transport goods from third countries;

  • cargo insurance.

Our advantages:

  • modern "green" truck park;

  • we use the satellite GPS system to communicate with the driver, accurate information about the location of the cargo is transmitted around the clock;

  • reliable insurance companies take out civilian insurance                      liability under the CMR Convention (Convention on International Contracts                      carriage of goods by road).
